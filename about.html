About Me

My name is Naeema Abdalla Ahmed Alnaqbi. I am a biomedical engineer working in emergency and ambulance services, and I am currently completing my MSc Artificial Intelligence at the University of Essex Online.

My core interest is integrating AI into healthcare in ways that improve patient safety, support clinicians, and respect ethical, legal and professional standards. The Research Methods and Professional Practice module helps me strengthen the academic skills needed to design, evaluate and communicate research responsibly.
