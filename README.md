## 1. Overview

This e-Portfolio presents a complete and structured record of my learning and assessment work for the
*Research Methods and Professional Practice* module.

It demonstrates my engagement with:

- Ethical and professional responsibilities in computing
- Research design and methodology
- Literature review development
- Quantitative and statistical analysis
- Collaborative academic discussion
- Professional development and reflective practice

All required unit activities (Units 1–12) are represented through written artefacts, reflections, analyses,
and supporting evidence, organised across interlinked HTML pages.
