Welcome to my e-Portfolio

This e-portfolio presents my work for the module Research Methods and Professional Practice on the MSc Artificial Intelligence at the University of Essex Online. It combines collaborative discussions, research-planning activities, statistics tasks and reflection, with a focus on my professional context as a biomedical engineer interested in AI for healthcare.
