Welcome to my e-Portfolio

Evidence of learning across Units 1–12 of the Research Methods and Professional Practice module.

This e-portfolio documents my learning journey throughout the Research Methods and Professional Practice module, part of my MSc Artificial Intelligence programme with the University of Essex Online. It brings together collaborative discussions, research design activities, statistics and data work, and reflective pieces from Units 1–12.

Each page in the navigation bar focuses on a specific strand of the module, with activities clearly labelled by unit number and assessment type. Evidence is presented alongside short explanations of what I did, why it mattered, and what I learned from it.
