Welcome to my e-Portfolio

This e-portfolio presents my completed work for the module Research Methods and Professional Practice on the MSc Artificial Intelligence at the University of Essex Online.

It brings together collaborative discussions, research-planning activities, statistical analysis tasks, and structured reflection, grounded in my professional context as a biomedical engineer with a strong interest in responsible AI in healthcare.

The portfolio provides an integrated, evidence-based record of learning across Units 1–12 and constitutes the final submission for the End of Module Assignment – Individual e-Portfolio.
