Literature Review & Proposal Outlines

Units 2–4 · Literature review and research proposal planning

My research interests sit at the intersection of artificial intelligence, healthcare and human physiology. The literature review activities in this module help me move from a broad curiosity about AI in healthcare to a focused, researchable topic that can support my long-term professional goals.

Early outline (Unit 2)

At the beginning of the module, my thinking is deliberately wide. I am exploring themes such as AI for clinical decision support, digital health records, triage systems in ambulance services, and the ethical risks of using sensitive physiological data. The early outline, when completed, will record how I mapped potential directions, identified key keywords and noted a small group of foundational papers.

Refined outline (Unit 4)

As the teaching moves towards Unit 4, the outline will become more precise. I expect to narrow towards a specific question, for example focusing on risk prediction, patient deterioration or responsible use of AI-generated recommendations in emergency care. Once the Unit 4 activity is finished, I will add the refined outline here, showing the shift from broad interest to clear research questions and objectives.

Until those units are complete, this section provides a structured place ready to hold my Unit 2 and Unit 4 literature review submissions.

Research Proposal Review (Unit 3)

Unit 3 · e-Portfolio Activity: Research Proposal Review

In Unit 3, I will participate in a formal peer-review exercise, reading a classmate’s draft proposal and offering feedback on clarity, feasibility, methodology and ethics. Once the activity is completed, this section will summarise:

  • The main strengths I identified in the proposal
  • Constructive suggestions I made to improve research design or ethics
  • What I learned about my own proposal by reviewing someone else’s work

For now, this card simply marks the location where the Unit 3 proposal review evidence will be added later in the module.

Literature Review Outline Submission (Unit 4)

Unit 4 · Finalised literature review structure

The Unit 4 literature review outline will provide the detailed structure for my reading: sections, themes, and key authors. When it is complete and submitted, I will copy the agreed outline into this section, highlighting how it supports the later research proposal.

At the current stage of the module, the final outline has not yet been written. This section is therefore reserved to hold that confirmed structure once it is available.

Proposal Outline (Unit 8)

Unit 8 · Consolidated research proposal

Future proposal evidence

By Unit 8, I will have developed a consolidated research proposal including a working title, research questions, objectives, methodology and ethical considerations. This section is prepared to hold that final outline once I reach Unit 8.

The finished content here will show how my ideas evolved from early literature mapping into a coherent, methodologically sound proposal that aligns with my professional interests in AI-enabled healthcare and emergency services.
