Literature Review & Proposal Outline

Unit 4 · Developing a focused research idea and initial literature mapping

This section is prepared for my Unit 4 literature review and research proposal outline. The work here will build on my interest in AI for healthcare and emergency settings, and will clarify:

  • The initial research topic and its relevance to AI and professional practice
  • Key themes and gaps identified in the existing literature
  • Preliminary research questions or objectives
  • Early thoughts on methodology and ethical considerations

As Unit 4 progresses, I will add structured notes from my literature search (e.g. databases used, search terms, inclusion/exclusion decisions) and a short, coherent outline of the proposed study. This will connect directly to the more refined proposal work in Units 7–8.

Unit 3 · Feedback and refinement of initial proposal ideas

In Unit 3, my draft research proposal will be shared for tutor and peer review. This section will document:

  • A brief description of the initial proposal submitted for review
  • Key feedback points from peers and/or the tutor
  • Changes I decide to make based on this feedback
  • How the review process improves the clarity, feasibility, and ethical robustness of the project

Final Proposal Outline

Unit 8 · Consolidated research proposal

By Unit 8, the aim is to present a more complete and coherent research proposal. This card is reserved for the final outline, which will typically include:

  • Background and rationale, linked to the literature review
  • Clear research aim(s) and research questions or hypotheses
  • Proposed methodology (e.g. qualitative, quantitative, mixed methods)
  • Sampling, data collection and data analysis plans
  • Ethical, legal and professional considerations, especially for AI and healthcare contexts

Once completed, this section will demonstrate that I can translate a broad interest (e.g. AI in healthcare or emergency response) into a rigorous, ethically aware research plan suitable for postgraduate study.
